Welcome to your
High School Band Page.

The High School Band is the highest level of instrumental performing ensemble at Forest Area.

High School band students will learn refinement and mastery of fundamentals and musicality from the Scott Rush book Habits of a Successful Musician.

Performances
The High School Band performs in both concert and marching venues
Marching performances include:
Home football games
The Cadillac Marching Review
The District Marching Expo at Thirlby Field.
In the spring, the Marching band performs at:
Trout Festival parade in Kalkaska
The 4th of July Parade in Fife Lake.
For Concert Band, the High School Band performs at:
Winter Concert
Spring Concert
District Festival
as well as, random events in Fife Lake, as requested by the community.
