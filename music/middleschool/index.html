Middle School Music Page
7th and 8th Grades.

Middle School Band is the first level of ensemble at Forest Area to participate in District Festival.

Students will learn advanced musical concepts and apply them in a performance setting.

We use the Scott Rush book Habits of a Successful Middle School Musician for fundamental work and warmups.

Middle School band students can participate in the marching band in the fall

8th graders will perform with the marching band in the spring at Trout Festival parade, and in the summer at the 4th of July Parade.

Students have the option to participate in Distric Solo and Ensemble Festival in the spring.

Performances:
Winter Concert
Spring Concert
District Festival

Middle School band students will help with High School Band and Choir performances such as parades and festivals.
